    Feminist tech products and projects

    There are a number of feminist tech products and projects that aim to
    promote gender equality and empower women in various ways

    Chayn

    A global volunteer-led open-source project that provides online resources for women experiencing domestic violence and abuse. Chayn provides users with a variety of keyword cards so that they can easily find the content they need, including everything from mental health and legal knowledge to financial support, and the overall page design uses gentle colors to put users in a more relaxed frame of mind, as well as equipped with a chatbot to address users' questions on time.

    Girls Who Code

    An international nonprofit organization that aims to close the gender gap in technology by teaching girls to code and providing them with the skills and resources they need to pursue careers in tech. They host different kinds of clubs, summer programs, and college & career programs to help girls in different ages and different situations learn more knowledge about programing.

    She++

    A student-run organization that aims to empower women in technology by providing them with resources and opportunities to succeed in the field. And She++ also seek to dismantle the untrue stereotype that computer science is not a career for women, and they collaborate with the technology industry to create a more appealing culture to women and dismantle the perception that women cannot succeed in this industry.

    Feminist Frequency

    Feminist Frequency: A video series and website that analyzes the representation of women in popular culture, including all kinds of video games, movies, and TV shows. Feminist Frequency aims to promote gender equality and challenge sexism and misogyny in media. By analyzing how feminist-related concepts are represented in movies, TV shows, and video games or presented in the opposite light, Feminist Frequency is trying to promote gender equality and challenge sexism and misogyny in the media.

    Feminist AI

    Feminist AI is a space created by Mutable Studio, a design and technology company that aims to integrate feminist principles into the development of AI technologies. Feminist AI involves working closely with diverse communities to co-create AI systems that reflect their values and needs. This includes using participatory design methods to involve marginalized communities in the design process, as well as applying a critical lens to issues such as data ethics and algorithmic accountability. Ultimately, the goal of Feminist AI is to create AI technologies that are more equitable, ethical, and just, and that contribute to a more equitable and sustainable future for all.

    FemLab

    FemLab is a research activist cooperative that focuses on the intersection of feminism and technology. The group's mission is to promote gender and diversity awareness in the field of technology and to encourage the development of more inclusive and equitable technology practices. And it has a wide range of activities including feminist AI, digital activism, gender and gaming, and the social impact of technology. The group also offers a number of educational programs and resources, including workshops, courses, and seminars on topics related to gender and technology.
