    The impact of technology on gender

    Gender bias and sexism are also prevalent issues in the technology sector.

    In short, technology may introduce gender bias and discrimination in some cases. We need

    to keep an eye on this issue and take steps to reduce gender bias and discrimination and

    create a more inclusive and just digital world.

    • Recruitment algorithms

      Many companies use algorithms
      to screen candidates.
      However, these algorithms may
      tend to target men as better
      candidates because they are trained based
      on historical data and bias.
      This can lead to discrimination against
      women and other minority
      groups in the hiring process.

    • Social media

      Social media is a public platform
      on which anyone can post
      content. However, some content
      on social media may involve
      sexism and gender harassment.
      In addition, social media
      may also reflect and reinforce
      gender stereotypes and
      gender binaries.

    • Digital safety

      Women and other minority groups
      are more vulnerable to attacks and
      harassment when it comes
      to online safety.
      For example, online violence
      against women (including harassment,
      threats and malicious behavior)
      is prevalent on social media.
